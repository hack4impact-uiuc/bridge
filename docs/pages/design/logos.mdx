#### Logo Usage

The Hack4Impact logo is made with a globe graphic and a logotype set in Chivo Regular font with a custom "4" and a custom “i”.

The horizontal logo is the primary logo and should be used in most instances.

The icon logo is the secondary logo and should be used in small-scale formats or in situations where the brand must be 
communicated quickly. An example of this situation would be social media profile photos.
